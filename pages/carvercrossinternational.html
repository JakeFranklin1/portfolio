About the Project

This is a professional landing page I developed for a recruitment agency specializing in satellite, space & defence, automotive, and advanced technologies industries. The project required a clean, corporate design that reflects the high-tech nature of their client base while maintaining professional credibility.

As the sole developer and designer, I worked closely with the client to understand their brand identity and target audience. The website features a modern, responsive design optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring potential candidates and clients can easily access information across all platforms.

The site was built with performance and SEO in mind, implementing best practices for fast load times and search engine visibility. I focused on creating an intuitive user experience that guides visitors through the agency's services, industry expertise, and contact information seamlessly.

This project showcases my ability to deliver professional, client-focused web solutions that meet business objectives while maintaining technical excellence and modern design standards.
